## 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1. **"Organization"** means the entity that collects, uses, or discloses Personal Information (equivalent to Data Controller).

1.2. **"Service Provider"** means the entity that processes Personal Information on behalf of the Organization (equivalent to Data Processor).

1.3. **"Personal Information"** means information about an identifiable individual.

1.4. **"PIPEDA"** means Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act.

1.5. **"Individual"** means the natural person to whom Personal Information relates.

---

## 2. CANADA-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS

### 2.1. PIPEDA Principles
Service Provider shall comply with PIPEDA's 10 Fair Information Principles:
1. **Accountability:** Designated individual responsible for compliance
2. **Identifying Purposes:** Process only for specified purposes
3. **Consent:** Obtain meaningful consent
4. **Limiting Collection:** Collect only necessary information
5. **Limiting Use, Disclosure, and Retention:** Use only for specified purposes
6. **Accuracy:** Ensure Personal Information is accurate
7. **Safeguards:** Implement appropriate security measures
8. **Openness:** Be transparent about privacy practices
9. **Individual Access:** Provide access to Personal Information
10. **Challenging Compliance:** Provide mechanism to challenge compliance

### 2.2. Consent
- **Meaningful Consent:** Obtain meaningful consent for processing
- **Withdrawal:** Individual may withdraw consent at any time
- **Consent Records:** Maintain records of consent

### 2.3. Individual Rights
Service Provider shall assist Organization in responding to Individual requests:
- Right to access Personal Information
- Right to challenge accuracy
- Right to request correction
- Right to file complaint with Privacy Commissioner

---

## 3. PROVINCIAL PRIVACY LAWS

### 3.1. Quebec (Law 25)
- **Privacy Officer:** Designate privacy officer
- **Privacy Impact Assessment:** Conduct PIAs for high-risk processing
- **Breach Notification:** Notify within 72 hours if high risk
- **Right to De-indexation:** Assist with de-indexation requests

### 3.2. British Columbia (PIPA)
- **Consent:** Obtain consent for collection, use, and disclosure
- **Access Requests:** Respond within 30 days
- **Breach Notification:** Notify Organization without undue delay

### 3.3. Alberta (PIPA)
- **Consent:** Obtain consent for collection, use, and disclosure
- **Access Requests:** Respond within 45 days
- **Breach Notification:** Notify Organization without undue delay

---

## 4. DATA LOCALIZATION

4.1. **Storage:** Personal Information may be stored:
- In Canada (preferred)
- Outside Canada with appropriate safeguards
- With consent or as required by law

4.2. **Cross-Border Transfer:** Any transfer outside Canada shall:
- Comply with PIPEDA requirements
- Use appropriate safeguards (encryption, contractual clauses)
- Obtain consent if required

---

## 5. BREACH NOTIFICATION

5.1. Service Provider shall:
- Notify Organization without undue delay (within 24 hours) of any breach
- Provide all information necessary for Organization to comply with breach notification requirements
- Assist Organization in notifying affected Individuals if required

5.2. **Quebec (Law 25):** Notify within 72 hours if:
- Breach poses high risk to Individuals
- Commission must be notified

---

## 6. AUDIT RIGHTS

6.1. Organization may:
- Request audit of Service Provider's compliance
- Request copies of security certifications
- Conduct on-site audits with 30 days' notice

---

## 7. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

7.1. **Retention:** Service Provider shall retain Personal Information only:
- For duration specified in Agreement
- As required by applicable law
- As necessary for Service Provider's legitimate business purposes

7.2. **Deletion:** Upon termination or request:
- Delete or return all Personal Information within 30 days
- Delete all copies, including backups, within 90 days
- Provide written confirmation of deletion
