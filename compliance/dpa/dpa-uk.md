## 2. UK-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS

### 2.1. UK GDPR Compliance
- Processor shall comply with UK GDPR requirements
- UK GDPR mirrors EU GDPR with UK-specific provisions
- ICO is the supervisory authority

### 2.2. Data Subject Rights
Processor shall assist Controller in responding to Data Subject requests under UK GDPR:
- Right of access (Article 15)
- Right to rectification (Article 16)
- Right to erasure (Article 17)
- Right to restriction of processing (Article 18)
- Right to data portability (Article 20)
- Right to object (Article 21)

### 2.3. Lawful Basis
Controller warrants it has lawful basis for processing under UK GDPR:
- Consent
- Contract
- Legal obligation
- Vital interests
- Public task
- Legitimate interests

## 3. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

### 3.1. UK to EEA Transfers
- **Adequacy Decision:** UK has adequacy decision for EEA transfers
- **Standard Contractual Clauses:** Use UK Addendum to EU SCCs

### 3.2. UK to Third Countries
- **Adequacy Decision:** Use if country has adequacy decision
- **Standard Contractual Clauses:** Use UK Addendum to EU SCCs
- **Transfer Impact Assessment:** Conduct TIA for high-risk transfers

### 3.3. UK Addendum
- Use UK Addendum to EU Standard Contractual Clauses
- Covers transfers from UK to third countries
- Maintained by ICO

---

## 4. DATA LOCALIZATION

4.1. **Storage:** Personal Data may be stored:
- In UK (preferred for UK residents)
- In EEA (adequate protection)
- In third countries (with appropriate safeguards)

4.2. **Processing:** Processing may occur:
- In UK
- In EEA
- In third countries (with appropriate safeguards)

---

## 5. BREACH NOTIFICATION

5.1. Processor shall:
- Notify Controller without undue delay (within 24 hours) of any Personal Data breach
- Provide all information necessary for Controller to comply with UK GDPR Article 33
- Assist Controller in notifying ICO and Data Subjects if required

5.2. **ICO Notification:**
- Notify ICO within 72 hours if high risk
- Provide detailed breach information
- Document breach and response

---

## 6. AUDIT RIGHTS

6.1. Controller may:
- Request audit of Processor's compliance
- Request copies of security certifications
- Conduct on-site audits with 30 days' notice

6.2. **ICO Audits:** Processor shall cooperate with ICO audits if required.

---

## 7. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

7.1. **Retention:** Processor shall retain Personal Data only:
- For duration specified in Agreement
- As required by applicable UK law
- As necessary for Processor's legitimate business purposes

7.2. **Deletion:** Upon termination or request:
- Delete or return all Personal Data within 30 days
- Delete all copies, including backups, within 90 days
- Provide written confirmation of deletion
