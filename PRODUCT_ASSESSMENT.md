## üéØ Product Classification

### **This is a HORIZONTAL SaaS Product, NOT Vertical**

#### Why It's Horizontal (Not Vertical):

1. **Industry-Agnostic Core Features**
   - Generic financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
   - Universal budgeting and forecasting
   - Standard financial calculations that work for any business
   - No industry-specific workflows or deep vertical integrations

2. **Industry Selection is Cosmetic**
   - Users can select industry (SaaS, E-commerce, Healthcare, etc.)
   - But the core functionality remains the same regardless of selection
   - Industry field is used for:
     - Organization metadata
     - Model categorization
     - Revenue model templates
   - **Not** for industry-specific features or workflows

3. **Universal Financial Operations**
   - All businesses need: revenue forecasting, expense management, cash flow
   - All businesses need: budgets, variance analysis, reporting
   - All businesses need: compliance, security, audit trails
   - These are horizontal needs, not vertical-specific

4. **No Deep Vertical Integration**
   - Missing industry-specific features like:
     - **Healthcare**: Patient billing, insurance claims, HIPAA workflows
     - **Retail**: Inventory management, POS integration, SKU tracking
     - **Manufacturing**: Production planning, supply chain, BOM
     - **Real Estate**: Property management, lease tracking, tenant billing
     - **Restaurants**: Menu costing, labor scheduling, tip management
